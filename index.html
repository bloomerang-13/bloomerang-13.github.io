The Many Hats of Analytics Leadership

Great analytics leaders wear many hats. Success requires the ability to move between technology, strategy, communication, and people leadership depending on what the moment requires.

My approach has always been to bring the right perspective to the right challenge — whether solving a technical implementation issue, guiding a team through change, or helping stakeholders discover the story within their data.

  • The Analyst Hat: Diving deep into analytics implementation, data quality, and technical problem-solving.
  • The Translator Hat: Connecting technical details with business outcomes and actionable insights.
  • The Leader Hat: Empowering teams, removing obstacles, and helping people grow.
  • The Speaker Hat: Sharing knowledge, building community, and making analytics approachable.
  • The Creator Hat: Exploring new ideas and building innovative solutions.
  • The Connector Hat: Creating relationships that bring teams together around a shared purpose.
